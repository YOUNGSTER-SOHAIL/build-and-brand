FAQ

Refund and Cancellation Policy?

We assume that you agree to our terms and conditions when placing an order. Once an order is placed, we do not offer any cancellation, exchange or returns. 

However, for any products in requirement of possible alterations, we will be happy to assist you.

Our packaging is done just as carefully as the quality of products they contain. Our team ensures that all products are released at a perfect condition from our end.

What if there’s a genuine defect /damage in the received product?

If there’s a genuine defect /damage in the received product caused in transit, a replacement will be offered only if there’s an uncut unpacking video within 24 hours of receiving it. Video should contain sealed packet with printed shipment label proof and product returned should contain original tags and packaging in an unworn/unused state.

On receipt of the returned product, our team will conduct a quality check and proceed to ship the replacement or refund. Store reserves the right to refuse a return if the product is found to be used and worn upon its arrival. Please note that the entire process of replacement or refund may take up to 12-15 days. Your cooperation and patience during the same will be highly valued.

What's your Shipping Policy?

We ship through registered and trusted courier partners for orders within India.

Please Note that Saturdays, Sundays, and Public Holidays are not set as working days for standard deliveries.

DOMESTIC SHIPPING:

We offer free shipping for all domestic orders. Delivery time for shipping is an estimated 3-10 days. The deliveries are dispatched to the shipping address recorded at checkout. All orders are processed from our warehouse in Jaipur.

Terms of Service?

EXCHANGE

We send all our products with love and precision, yet if you happen to receive the wrong size then you can send us an exchange request at adaajaipur4india@gmail.com . An executive will respond to your request within working hours.

Customers need to self-ship the product to us. Exchange pieces in the correct size will be made as soon as possible. You will receive the shipping information on your registered mail id once it is dispatched.

Please make sure that when the product is being exchanged:

  • Exchange will only be done for sizing issues or defective items.
  • Exchange request shall be raised within 24 hours of delivery. After that, we will not be able to entertain any requests.
  • Customers need to self-ship the product to us within 3 days after the delivery.
  • Exchange will be done only once for one piece.
  • All product tags should be intact and in their original packing with an invoice.
  • Product must be in an unused, unwashed, and undamaged condition.
  • A confirmation email will be sent to you once the shipment is received at our warehouse and checked for quality.
  • In case, the product fails our quality inspection, the product will be shipped back to you.
  • We do not have a reverse pickup facility for now. Please do not forget to take an acknowledgment receipt from the courier person about the number of products you are dispatching with his signature and cell phone number. We will not be responsible for any lost shipment.
